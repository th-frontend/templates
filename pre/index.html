What to expect with your appointment

At [company], we're more than just a contractor. We're your partner in protecting what matters most. Watch this video to learn exactly what to expect during your home inspection.

Here's what happens with your scheduled inspection

1 | Confirmation call

A few days prior to your inspection, your Design Specialist will call you to introduce themselves and confirm the date and time.

2 | On-the-way call

On the day of your inspection, your Design Specialist will call you to let you know they're on the way to your home. They'll also send you a GPS tracking link so you'll know exactly when you can expect them to arrive.

3 | Inspection

Upon arrival, your Design Specialist will introduce themselves and answer any questions you have. They'll conduct a thorough inspection of the issue or problem area. We encourage you to join them during the inspection and ask questions.

4 | Repair plan

Your Design Specialist will sit down with you to explain what caused the problem, how we can fix it, and discuss your options and a plan for repairing your home. By the end of this phase, you'll know exactly what's causing your home's problems, exactly how we can fix it, and exactly how much it will cost.

5 | Scheduling the repair

Once you're ready to move forward with the work, your Design Specialist will coordinate with you to schedule the project so you can get the peace of mind you deserve as soon as possible.
